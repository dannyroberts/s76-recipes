Ingredients
-----------
* 6 dried Chinese mushrooms
* 6 cups chicken stock
* 1 diced firm tofu OR 1/4 lb. lean boneless pork cut into thin stripes
* 1 tbsp ginger root, grated
* 1/2 cup julienned bamboo shoots
* 3 tbsp rice vinegar
* 2 tbsp (sodium-reduced) soy sauce
* 1 tbsp dark sesame oil
* 1 tsp chili paste (optional)
* 2 tbsp corn starch
* 1/2 teaspoon sugar
* 2 green onions, sliced

Preparation
-----------
Soak dried mushrooms in warm water for 15 min, drain. Remove and discard stems, cut them into thin slices. In large saucepan bring stock to boil. Add mushrooms and pork and ginger, simmer for 10 min. Add bamboo shoots and tofu. In a small bowl combine the other things, stirring in corn starch and sugar until smooth. Stir this stuff into the soup and boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes. Sprinkle each serving with green onions.
