Ingredients
-----------
* 4 ripe bananas, smashed
* 1/4 cup melted butter
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1 beaten egg 
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1 pinch salt
* 1.5 cups flour
* 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Preparation
-----------
Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. With a wooden spoon, mix butter into the mashed bananas in a large mixing bowl. Mix in the sugar, egg, and vanilla. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture and mix in. Add the flour last, mix. Pour mixture into a buttered 4x8 inch loaf pan. Bake for 1 hour. Cool on a rack. Remove from pan and slice to serve.
