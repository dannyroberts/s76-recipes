Ingredients
-----------
* n eggs, where n is the number of people
* 2n matzos
* olive oil
* salt

Preparation
-----------
Soak the matzos in warm water (milk is also a possibility) for a minute, and then squeeze away the water. Grease a pan and use the greased pan to brown the matzos. Then add some salt. Add the eggs and scramble until cooked but not dry.
