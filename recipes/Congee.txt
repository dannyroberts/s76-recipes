Ingredients
-----------
* n rice-cups rice (where a rice-cup is the little scoop thing, not 1 cup)
* n cups of water
* something salty (soup stalk, dumpling juices, etc)
* additional salt
* other toppings: kimchee, meat, tofu, mushrooms, spring onions, hundred-year-old eggs (皮蛋）, etc.
	* balls
	* other balls

Preparation
-----------
Put the rice and the water into a pot on high heat until boiling. Once boiling commences, reduce to medium-low heat and add the other ingredients. Cook for 30 minutes or more.
