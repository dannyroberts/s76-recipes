Ingredients
-----------
* 2 cups all-purpose flour
* 2 cups yellow cornmeal
* 1 cup white sugar
* 2 teaspoon salt 
* 2 tbsp baking powder
* 2 eggs
* 2 cups milk
* 1/2 cup vegetable oil or butter

Preparation
-----------
Preheat oven to 400 F. Spray or lightly grease a baking sheet at least that is 2 inches deep. In a large bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt and baking powder. Stir in egg, milk and vegetable oil until well combined. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
