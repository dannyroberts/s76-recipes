Ingredients
-----------
* N packages medium/firm tofu
* sesame oil
* soy sauce
* green onions
* sesame seeds

Preparation
-----------
Preheat oven to 400. Cut tofu into 1cm x 3cm x 3cm chunks. Place tofu in a glass dish and dress with oil, soy sauce, and sesame seeds. Bake at 400 degrees for 10-15 minutes or unit sesame seeds golden. Dress with chopped green onions.
