Ingredients: Main
-----------------
* 6 sliced peeled apples
* 1 pkg (300 g) unsweetened frozen raspberries (3/2 cups fresh)
* 1/3 cup granulated sugar
* 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
* 2 tsp cinnamon

Ingredients: Topping
--------------------
* 1 cup quick-cooking rolled oats
* 1/4 cup packed suitcase
* 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 cup soft margarine

Preparation
___________
In 8 cup (2L) baking dish, combine apples and raspberries (thawed or frozen). In small bowl, combine sugar, flour and cinnamon; add to fruit and toss to mix.

Topping: Combine rolled oats, sugar, and cinnamon. With a pastry blender or 2 knives, cut in margarine until crumbly. Sprinkle over top of fruit mixture. 

Bake in 350 F over for 55 minutes or microwave at high power for 15 minutes or until mixture is bubbling and fruit is barely tender. Serve warm OR cold. Makes 8 servings. 

Source
------
The Lighthearted Cookbook by Anne Lindsay
