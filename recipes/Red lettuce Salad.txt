Ingredients
-----------
* Red lettuce
* Small cucumbers
* Tomatoes on vine
* Broccoli
* Onion (optional)
* Fresh basil, dill, etc.
* Olive oil, balsamic vinegar, white vinegar (2:2:1)
* Feta cheese

Preparation
-----------
Cut the relevant things, and make a dressing out of the oil and vinegar.
