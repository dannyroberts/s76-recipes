Ingredients
-----------
* Chinese eggplants
* Olive oil
* Lemon juice
* Salt AND pepper
* Garlic
* Parsley

Preparation
-----------
Cut the eggplants in half the long way. Brush on some olive oil, and bake them (400 F) face-down for about half an hour or until soft. Take them out and flip 'em over. Put on a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper, and garlic. Garnish with parsley. Refrigerate and serve cold or at room temperature.
