Ingredients
-----------
* 1/4 cup soy sauce
* 1 + 1/4 cup water
* 3 tbsp brown sugar
* ~1 tbsp fresh grated ginger
* 1 clove minced garlic 
* 2 tbsp cornstarch or potato starch
* 1 hint cooking sake and/or mirin (yellow liquid in a bottle)
 
Preparation
-----------
Combine 1 cup water, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger into a saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Then dissolve cornstarch in 1/4 cup cold water and add this to the boiling mixture. Stir constantly, allowing the sauce to thicken. If the sauce is too thick, add a little water or soy sauce.
