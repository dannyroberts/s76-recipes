Ingredients
-----------
* 2 cans black beans
* 1 onion
* a few cloves of garlic
* 2 peppers (red, orange, yellow)
* 2 mangos
* soy sauce
* w-long-name sauce

Preparation
___________
Brown the onions and garlic in an existing pan. Cut the peppers into 1.5-inch-long strips and add them to the existing onion-garlic-pan mixture. After a few minutes, add the beans and stir for a couple minutes. Add soy sauce and w-long-name-sauce and cover. Simmer for a few (<5) minutes. Add mango and mix. Cover again for a few (<5) minutes. Then serve warm.
